## What Even Is RackNerd?

RackNerd is a US-based IaaS (Infrastructure-as-a-Service) provider founded by Dustin B. Cisneros. They do KVM VPS, AMD Ryzen VPS, Windows VPS, shared hosting, dedicated servers, and colocation. As of 2026, they operate out of 21 data centers across 20 locations — Los Angeles, Dallas, Chicago, New York, New Jersey, Seattle, Atlanta, San Jose, Ashburn, Miami, Tampa, Montreal, Toronto, Amsterdam, London, Frankfurt, Strasbourg, Dublin, Singapore, and Utah.

They made the Inc. 5000 list of fastest-growing private US companies in both 2024 and 2025. Which is kind of remarkable for a company whose pitch is basically "we're very cheap and also very good."

The infrastructure is built on Intel Xeon processors, RAID-10 protected pure SSD storage, and a network optimized by Noction IRP — which is smart routing technology that automatically finds the best available path for your traffic. DDoS protection is included. Every KVM VPS gets a dedicated IPv4, full root access, and 1Gbps network speeds.
